# Deep Case Study Synthesis & Scoping Analysis

**Document Status:** DRAFT | **Author:** Ace (Architect/BusinessManager) | **Context:** AI Workflow Pivot (Deep Integration)

## 1. The Core Offerings (What we can offer & to whom)

Based on the expert modeling of InfyOm's successful cases (FlexyFit & WholeTex), we must split the D-Tul service architecture into two distinct, highly-profitable pillars. We are not selling "software"—we are selling **Latency Elimination and Structural Stability**.

### Pillar A: "The Operational Workflow Engine" (Automation-First)
- **What it is:** Pure webhook and API-driven automation bridging existing legacy chaos.
- **The Ideal Target (Who):** Scaling B2B Wholesalers, Lead-Gen Agencies, and Mid-Market Distributors (10-50 employees). Companies flooded with unstructured data (WhatsApp inquiries, Excel ledgers).
- **The Pitch:** "We eliminate your manual bottlenecks. Auto-triage inquiries, real-time ledgers, 0-latency follow-ups."
- **The Tech Stack:** n8n (or Make), WhatsApp Business API, standard REST APIs, lightweight UI dashboards (Bun/Vite) if necessary. Minimal custom coding; maximum logic mapping.

### Pillar B: "The Infrastructure Refactor" (Architecture-First)
- **What it is:** Teardown of fracturing legacy monoliths (PHP, old WordPress) and rebuilding into highly scalable, component-driven architectures.
- **The Ideal Target (Who):** EdTech platforms, enterprise SaaS, or established institutions with 10k+ concurrent users suffering from database lag or multi-tenant (multi-site) structural failure.
- **The Pitch:** "Your foundation cannot support your next 10x growth. We re-architect for scale."
- **The Tech Stack:** Strict, modern ecosystems. Bun, Vite, React (Frontend), modern robust backend architectures (Node.js/Bun microservices).

## 2. Economics & Delivery (How much & How long)

### For Pillar A (Operational Workflow Engine)
*Example: The WholeTex B2B Automation.*
- **Time Estimation:** 1 to 4 Weeks. (The strength of this pillar is high-speed deployment).
- **Pricing Matrix:**
  - **Consultation & Logic Map (The Hook):** $500 - $1,000 (We map their chaos into a structured JSON/Mermaid logic flow).
  - **Implementation Setup:** $2,500 - $8,000 (Depending on the number of systems integrated).
  - **Monthly Retainer (Ops/Maintenance):** $500 - $1,500/mo.
- **Why this works:** The client measures the cost against human labor (e.g., 60 hours/month saved = thousands of dollars recovered). The ROI is immediate.

### For Pillar B (Infrastructure Refactor)
*Example: FlexyFit Academy PHP to Laravel Migration.*
- **Time Estimation:** 8 to 24+ Weeks. (This is a deep, risky, surgical operation).
- **Pricing Matrix:**
  - **Architectural Audit (The Hook):** $2,000 - $5,000.
  - **Full Migration Build:** $20,000 - $75,000+.
  - **SLA & Maintenance:** $2,000+/mo.
- **Why this works:** The client is bleeding users and revenue due to site failure. They are paying for survival and the ability to scale globally (e.g., entering 12+ new countries).
